First Edition - Free for All Attendees!

PyCon Togo 2025 will be our first edition and completely free for all participants. Your sponsorship directly helps us create an accessible event that builds Togo's tech community from the ground up.

Why Sponsor PyCon Togo?

PyCon Togo is the first Python conference in Togo, aiming to bring together developers, students, and tech enthusiasts from across the region. As a new community, we need your support to make this event successful and establish a foundation for future growth.

Benefits for Sponsors

  • Be recognized as an early supporter of Togo's Python community
  • Connect with tech talent in an emerging market
  • Demonstrate your commitment to tech education in West Africa
  • Gain visibility for your brand among developers and students
  • Help shape the future of the tech ecosystem in Togo
  • Network with other forward-thinking companies and organizations
